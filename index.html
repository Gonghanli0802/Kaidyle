Kaidyle International Trade Inc

Canadian Foundation. Global Impact. Unwavering Dedication to Trusted Partnerships and Trades.

Kaidyle International Trade Inc, located in Ontario, Canada, is a company engaged in domestic and international trade. Primarily serving clients in Canada, our commitment to quality and service allows us to build trust and foster lasting relationships with our customers, enabling us to excel in both domestic and international markets.

International Trading Business

The international trade division primarily focuses on the export of agricultural products, including Canadian white honey, flax oil, coconut oil, and more. Currently, our exports are destined for China, with plans to expand to other Asian countries in the future. This expansion demonstrates our dedication to growing our global presence and meeting the needs of diverse markets.

Wonderbrands Partner

Domestic trade is a key development goal for the company. We are a distributor for WonderBrands, one of Canada's largest bread suppliers. At present, we manage three distribution routes for the company, with an annual sales of $3,000,000.00. Our partnership with WonderBrands highlights our commitment to supporting local businesses and providing high-quality products to our customers.
